About Us

GreenSetGo is a sustainability and climate solutions company helping businesses transition toward low-carbon and net-zero futures. We partner with advanced technology platforms to deliver accurate carbon accounting, ESG reporting, and verified carbon credit generation.

We specialise in end-to-end carbon projects — reforestation, biochar, renewable energy, and nature-based solutions that generate certified carbon credits. Our approach blends science, technology, and execution to help companies reduce emissions, meet regulatory requirements, and unlock new revenue opportunities through carbon markets.

Our Solutions

Carbon Accounting

We help businesses accurately measure their Scope 1, Scope 2, and Scope 3 emissions using advanced AI-powered tools and automated data collection.

Our carbon accounting process aligns with GHG Protocol, ISO 14064, and global best practices, giving your organisation a clear, audit-ready emissions baseline.

What you get:
  • Detailed carbon footprint report
  • Automated emissions tracking
  • Activity data mapping & verification
  • Real-time dashboards (powered by AI & MRV tools)
  • Compliance-ready documentation (BRSR, CDP, CBAM)

ESG Solutions

We design tailored Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) strategies to help organisations meet regulatory requirements, improve brand credibility, and achieve long-term resilience.

Our team aligns your ESG framework with SEBI BRSR, GRI, SASB, and global sustainability reporting standards.

What we deliver:
  • ESG diagnostics & gap analysis
  • BRSR & sustainability reporting automation
  • Materiality assessment & stakeholder engagement
  • Risk management & compliance roadmap
  • Policy formulation (environment, safety, governance, DEI, etc.)

Net Zero Strategy

We help organisations build a clear, actionable, and science-aligned path to reach carbon neutrality and net-zero targets.

Our strategies combine technology-driven emissions reduction with nature-based solutions and high-quality offsets.

What we deliver:
  • Science-based target setting (SBTi aligned)
  • Emissions reduction roadmap
  • Renewable energy & efficiency solutions
  • Carbon neutrality planning
  • Tracking & reporting through AI-driven MRV systems

Carbon Credit Validation and Verification

We develop and manage high-quality carbon offset projects, enabling companies to generate, verify, and monetise carbon credits through nature-based and technology-based solutions such as reforestation, biochar, renewable energy, and waste-to-value projects.

What we provide:
  • Project design & feasibility assessment
  • Carbon sequestration modelling
  • Registration under Verra, Gold Standard, CDM, or Indian carbon market
  • MRV (Monitoring, Reporting & Verification)
  • Third-party validation
